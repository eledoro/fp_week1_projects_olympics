Tokyo Olympics

History

The Olympic Games began over 2,700 years ago in Olympia and occurred every four years. Around 50,000 people came from all over the Greek world to watch and take part. Winners were given a wreath of leaves and a hero's welcome back home instead of medals. Athletes competed for the glory of their city and winners were seen as being touched by the gods.
